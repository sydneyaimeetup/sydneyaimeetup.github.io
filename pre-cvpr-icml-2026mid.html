Overview

A focused seminar sharing and discussing the latest breakthrough papers from top-tier machine learning and computer vision conferences.

Join us for a dynamic research sharing session spotlighting cutting-edge papers from CVPR 2026 and ICML 2026. This event brings together rising researchers from universities across the greater Sydney area to present and discuss their latest contributions in machine learning and computer vision. It offers a unique opportunity to engage with the vibrant local research community.
